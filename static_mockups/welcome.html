Hunt

Scour is a new way for individuals to partake in real life treasure hunts. We create custom hunts with specialized clues that will lead to the coveted treasure.

Cash-in

Completing a hunt is more than just a moral victory – there are prizes to be won! From certificates to products to cold hard cash – your efforts will be rewarded! But be sure to hurry – there isn’t enough treasure for everyone!

Market

Scour provides a fun way for organizations to raise awareness and/or market new products. Each hunt is tailored specifically for each case.
